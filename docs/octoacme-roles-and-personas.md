# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers create intuitive, accessible, and delightful user experiences. They conduct user research, design interfaces, and collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to ensure solutions meet user needs and usability standards.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Establish and maintain design systems and guidelines
- Collaborate with Product Managers on feature scope and prioritization
- Work with Developers to ensure designs are implemented accurately
- Advocate for user needs and accessibility standards

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered solutions that drive adoption and satisfaction
- Reduce support burden through intuitive design
- Maintain consistency across products and platforms

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with stakeholders and developers
- Usability research findings and recommendations
- Design critiques and feedback sessions
- Weekly syncs with Product Managers and engineering leads

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove team blockers, and coach the organization in agile best practices. They serve the team and organization to improve delivery cadence and team effectiveness.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove impediments and blockers that slow team progress
- Coach team members on agile practices and mindset
- Monitor sprint health and velocity trends
- Escalate cross-team dependencies and organizational challenges
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement

### Goals
- Maintain consistent sprint cadence and predictability
- Maximize team velocity and productivity
- Build a psychologically safe, high-performing team
- Reduce organizational friction and delays

### Typical Communication
- Agile ceremonies and sprint metrics
- Blocker escalations and coaching conversations
- Retrospective action item tracking
- Collaboration with Project Managers on scheduling and resource planning

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business stakeholders and the technical team. They gather, document, and clarify requirements to ensure alignment and reduce ambiguity.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct stakeholder interviews and gather requirements
- Document functional and non-functional requirements clearly
- Clarify acceptance criteria and edge cases with stakeholders and Developers
- Identify and communicate dependencies and scope changes
- Validate solutions against original requirements
- Maintain requirements traceability and documentation

### Goals
- Reduce rework and scope creep through clear requirements
- Accelerate delivery by preventing misalignment
- Increase stakeholder confidence in solutions
- Enable consistent communication across teams

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops and stakeholder interviews
- Detailed requirement specs and acceptance criteria
- Change requests and scope clarification discussions
- Collaboration with Product Managers and Developers during planning and execution

---

## Customer Support Liaison

### Role Summary
Customer Support Liaisons represent the voice of the customer within the development cycle. They bring customer feedback, pain points, and usage data into prioritization and product decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Collect and synthesize customer feedback from support channels
- Identify high-impact bugs and feature requests based on customer volume and severity
- Provide customer context for prioritization decisions
- Participate in retrospectives to share customer impact insights
- Communicate product updates and workarounds to support teams
- Validate solutions with customer use cases and pain points

### Goals
- Reduce customer-impacting bugs through early feedback
- Prioritize work that delivers the most customer value
- Improve support efficiency and customer satisfaction
- Bridge communication between support and engineering

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries and impact analysis
- Weekly syncs with Product Managers on customer priorities
- Support team briefings and release notes
- Collaboration with Developers to validate bug fixes and features

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment processes. They ensure reliable, secure, and efficient delivery of software to production and enable teams to deploy with confidence.

### Responsibilities
- Design, build, and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure and deployment environments
- Implement security, monitoring, and observability standards
- Automate routine operational tasks
- Support incident response and rollback procedures
- Collaborate with Developers on deployment best practices and troubleshooting

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and safe deployments
- Reduce manual effort and deployment risk
- Maintain high system availability and security
- Provide clear visibility into system health and performance

### Typical Communication
- CI/CD pipeline setup and troubleshooting with Developers
- Deployment coordination and release notes
- Infrastructure and security reviews
- Incident response and post-mortems
- Weekly syncs on infrastructure health and deployment processes

## Cross-Functional Collaboration Matrix

| Role | Primary Dependencies | Communication Frequency | Key Interactions |
|------|----------------------|------------------------|-------------------|
| **Developer** | Developers, QA, DevOps | Daily | Code reviews, standups, design discussions |
| **Product Manager** | Stakeholders, Business Analyst, UX Designer | Weekly | Roadmap alignment, prioritization, requirements |
| **Project Manager** | All roles | Weekly+ | Status updates, risk management, scheduling |
| **UX Designer** | Product Manager, Developers, Business Analyst | 2–3x weekly | Design reviews, requirements validation, usability feedback |
| **Scrum Master** | All roles | Daily | Ceremonies, blocker removal, process coaching |
| **Business Analyst** | Product Manager, Stakeholders, Developers | 2–3x weekly | Requirements clarification, scope validation |
| **Customer Support Liaison** | Product Manager, Developers, QA | Weekly | Customer feedback, prioritization, release communication |
| **DevOps Engineer** | Developers, Project Manager, QA | As needed + weekly check-ins | Deployment coordination, infrastructure setup, incident response |
